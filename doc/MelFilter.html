MelFilter

One of the types of objects in PRAAT.

An object of type MelFilter represents an acoustic time-frequency representation of a sound: the power spectral density P(f, t), expressed in dB's. It is sampled into a number of points around equally spaced times ti and frequencies fj (on a Mel frequency scale).

Inside a MelFilter
