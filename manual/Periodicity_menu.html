Periodicity menu

A menu that occurs in the Dynamic menu for a Sound.

This menu contains commands for analysing the pitch contour of the selected Sound:

Sound: To Pitch...
Sound: To Pitch (ac)...
Sound: To Pitch (cc)...
Sound: To Harmonicity (cc)...
Sound: To Harmonicity (ac)...
