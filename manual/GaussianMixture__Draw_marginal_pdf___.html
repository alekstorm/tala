GaussianMixture: Draw marginal pdf...

A command to draw the marginal p.d.f. (probability density function) of the selected GaussianMixture.

A marginal distribution is the projection of the (multivariate) p.d.f. on one dimension or direction. This direction may also be externally defined by selecting a PCA and a GaussianMixture together.
