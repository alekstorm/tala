Sound: To Harmonicity (cc)...

A command that creates a Harmonicity object from every selected Sound object.

Purpose

to perform a short-term HNR analysis.

Algorithm

The algorithm performs an acoustic periodicity detection on the basis of a forward cross-correlation analysis. For information on the settings, see Sound: To Harmonicity (ac)....
