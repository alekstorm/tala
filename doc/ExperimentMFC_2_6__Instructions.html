ExperimentMFC 2.6. Instructions

Before the experiment begins, the listener will see the startText in the centre of the screen. During each trial, she will see the runText at the top of the screen. During breaks, she will see the pauseText in the centre of the screen. After all the trials have been performed, she will see the endText. As you can see in the example, all these texts can consist of multiple lines.
