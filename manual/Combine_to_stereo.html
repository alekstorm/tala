Combine to stereo

To combine two mono Sound objects into one single stereo Sound, select the two Sounds in the list and choose Combine to stereo from the Combine sounds menu. A new stereo Sound will appear in the list.

Of the two original mono Sounds, the one that is higher in the list will become the left channel of the new stereo Sound, and the one that is lower in the list will become the right channel of the new Sound.
